Bruce Forey is recognized as a dynamic and accomplished marketing and communications professional. He offers a track record within the broadband industry for developing and deploying strategic marketing plans, designing and launching effective PR campaigns, and delivering communications initiatives that drive brand awareness, boost sales, and advance the organizational mission. Before establishing the Broadmax Group, Forey served 12 years at utility broadband cooperatives establishing marketing and communications departments.
At GVTC Communications, Forey led the company’s first communications department organized to support fiber broadband and other business operations. He developed a campaign to educate members, community leaders, employees and the media about the many benefits of fiber broadband compared to older technologies. This strategy led to increased brand awareness that helped GVTC successfully compete against Fortune 50 internet providers for customers. Forey also collaborated closely with executive management to shape and deliver strategic messaging for the company’s annual meetings and employee town halls. In addition, He led the company into social media and guided GVTC’s entry into digital advertising which contributed to a 30% increase in broadband sales during its first year of activity. He also served as an eight-year board member for The GVTC Foundation directing an awareness drive that contributed to the nonprofit’s significant growth in contributions and giving. Bruce is accredited in public relations (APR) and participates in many training sessions offered by the American Marketing Association. His community involvement also includes the Society of St. Vincent de Paul and serving as a 2019 Boerne Chamber of Commerce board of director.
