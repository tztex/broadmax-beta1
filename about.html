Broadmax Team

Bruce Forey and Tom Zanoli


BroadMax Group stems from two careers focused on moving fiber broadband forward. Bruce Forey and Tom Zanoli were longtime team members of a Texas telephone cooperative, also considered pioneers in fiber broadband service. Bruce and Tom learned a lot about leveraging fiber technology to compete with Fortune 50 companies, generate new revenue streams, build a brand and much more. Their contributions in product development, marketing and communications strategies helped the company achieve impressive revenues. They also know what challenges can happen when a company expands into a totally new service. Becoming a broadband provider is a huge paradigm shift for a telephone or electric utility. Bruce and Tom understand these obstacles very well and formed BroadMax Group. Their mission is to offer new and established internet providers valuable industry insight combined with forward-thinking solutions.
