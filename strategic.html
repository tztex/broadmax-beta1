Strategic Services


Market Research:

Knowledge is power. Doing the upfront work to truly understand your market, customers, along with their perceptions of your company is necessary for any type of strategic planning. Broadmax collects the information and delivers valuable insight to help with all phases of your broadband operational planning.


SWOT

Part of the research process, mapping out your Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats, is key to developing the overall marketing strategy. Broadmax is the resource to objectively gather and present the facts, along with strategic solutions.


Positioning

Your organization has key strengths that may not be consistently communicated. Broadmax uses a proven system to clearly identify what makes you great. We take the findings to create a strong positioning statement for your organization. The next step is to continually incorporate elements of your positioning statement into marketing and communication. Do this and customers will clearly know what sets you apart from the competition.


Change Management

We’ve personally experienced the operational and communication challenges organizations face when expanding into broadband. There’s a lot for employees to take in with this paradigm shift. A strategic communication plan is key to keeping your team informed and engaged. Let our track record helping organizations successfully transition during this critical time be of assistance to your organization. Whether it’s an audit or comprehensive approach, BroadMax can help.


Business planning
